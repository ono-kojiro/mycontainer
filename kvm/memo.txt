List OS variant
$ osinfo-query os

List domains

$ virsh list --all


Change Max Memory Size

$ virsh dumpxml netbsd8
$ virsh dominfo netbsd8
$ virsh setmaxmem netbsd8 2048M


Show DHCP address of domains

$ virsh net-dhcp-leases default

Show Bridge Information

$ brctl show
